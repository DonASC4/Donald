Chronology of Junji Ito

1987
    Junji ito publishes "Tomie" in Monthly Halloween and later wins the Kazuo Umezu award.
1988
    Ito publishes his second work titled "Flesh-colored horror"
1998
    Ito's third, and arguably most well known, work named "Uzumaki" is published in a weekly manga magazine called Big Comic spirits and ran until 1999. It has had various re-releases since.
