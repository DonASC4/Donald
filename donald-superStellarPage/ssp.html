Story

The story of NieR takes place in the far future, where a young boy named Nier is trying his hardest to find a cure for his sister's sickness, called the black scrawl. The entire village knows of young Yonah's illness, and lends their support by hiring Nier for numerous odd jobs and errands. Some would say the work is beneath a man of his caliber, but he holds his head high and does whatever he can to provide for his sister, all the while hoping that the village's wisest member, Popola, and her twin sister Devola can discover a cure. Along the way he meets a talking magic wielding book named Grimoire Weiss, a foul mouthed hot headed woman named Kaine, and a mysterious young boy named Emil.

Characters

Nier

Nier serves as the protagonist of the orignal Nier game. He lives to help his younger sister, Yonah, find a cure for her disease called the "Black Scrawl". He lives in his former parent's house, taking odd jobs around town to earn money.

Kaine

Kaine is another companion of Nier, who joins him after he helps her in the killing of a shade named Hook. She has a disposition of angry and cruel, which is partly the result of her being possessed by a shade herself, albeit the fact that more often than not she is shy and collected.

Grimoire Weiss

Weiss is Nier's companion who helps him out on his quest to save Yonah. He wields magic gives Nier access to magical spells known as "Words" and various weapon upgrades.

Emil

Emil is the fourth and final character in Nier's party. He is cursed (or as otherwise reassured by the party, blessed) with the ability to turn anyone who sees his eyes to stone. Emil reveals himself to be No. 7, an ancient weapon created by magical experimentation.
